Beyond
The Stack

The same curiosity that drives my research into Multi-Agentic AI and security systems spills over into how I see the world visually. Design isn't decoration — it's the layer that makes complex ideas accessible.

Whether it's crafting a pitch deck that secures a regional finalist spot or photographing the mountain city I call home, I believe the best technical communicators are also visual thinkers.

This page is the honest, unpolished side — works in progress, experiments, and the things I make just because they're interesting.

Illustration & Drawing

Character sketches, concept art, and ink studies. Mostly analog — sometimes digital.

Graphic Design

Visual identity, presentation design, and editorial layouts. Canva, Figma, and the occasional hand-lettered piece.

Photography

Documenting the Cordillera region, tech communities, and the quiet moments between events.

Motion & Animation

Interface motion, animated posters, and short-form visual experiments.
